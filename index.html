What is wind chill?

A wind chill index accounts for a combination of air temperature and how fast the wind is blowing. Higher winds strip heat away from the body more quickly.

Formula of Windchill = 13.12 + 0.6215T – 11.37 (V^0.16) + 0.3965T (V^0.16)
